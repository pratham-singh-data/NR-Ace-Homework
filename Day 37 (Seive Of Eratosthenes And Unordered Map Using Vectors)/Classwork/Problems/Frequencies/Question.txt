Use unordered map:
    You will be given n integers
    then a number k
    u have to print all those unique numbers whose frequency is > k
    output should be in a sorted order
    eg:
        input:  10
                1 5 1 3 1 2 2 6 5 3
                3
        output: 1

        input:  10
                1 5 1 3 1 2 2 6 5 3
                2
        output: 1 2 3 5
